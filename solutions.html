Solutions

We require numerous actions to address the four main categories of pollution: air, water, land, and sound. Tough regulations, clean technology, eco-friendly practices, and more public awareness are all necessary. This can protect the environment and maintain our health.

Solutions for Air Pollution

Switch to clean power like wind and solar.

Make car and factory smog rules tough. Boost buses, trains, and electric cars use.

Plant trees and make green areas to suck up dirt in air.

Tell people why cutting air dirt matters.

Solutions for Water Pollution

Fix dirty water systems to stop farm and factory leak

Make rules on trash in water strict.

Back up saving rivers, lakes, and seas.

Teach folks how to throw away stuff right and reuse.

Solutions for Soil Pollution

Farm in ways that keep poisons and plant food out of water.

Use farming steps like changing crops and making flat steps on hills to save soil. Clean up dirty lands.

Push farming without harsh chemicals. Watch factories to keep dirt out of ground.

Solutions for Plastic Pollution

Plastic trash piles up in our world, including seas, rivers, beaches, and on land.

This happens when we don't dispose of plastic right, don't recycle enough, and use too much throw-away plastic.

Plastic waste harms sea life, animals, and lands, as creatures eat or get caught in plastic stuff, causing hurt, lack of air, and death.

Also, tiny plastic bits can get into what we eat, which may harm our health.
